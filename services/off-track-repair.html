Off-Track Door Repair

Door jumped off the rails? We'll get it back on track safely - fast response across PA, NJ, and DE. Call now or request service online.

Request Service Call Now

Signs your door is off track

  • Door moves unevenly or at an angle
  • Loud scraping or grinding when opening
  • Door stuck partway - won't open or close fully
  • Visible gap between rollers and track
  • Cables hanging loose or appear slack

Important: Stop using the door immediately. Forcing an off-track door can bend the track permanently or snap the cables.

How we fix it

  • Inspect tracks, rollers, cables, and hardware
  • Carefully guide door back onto the track
  • Realign and secure the track if bent or shifted
  • Replace damaged rollers or cables as needed
  • Test full door travel and balance before leaving

We address the root cause - not just the symptom - so the door stays on track.

Common causes we see

  • Broken spring that caused the door to drop suddenly
  • Snapped cable on one side
  • Worn or cracked rollers that slipped out
  • Track shifted from impact or loose bolts
  • Repeated use with a failing spring over time
