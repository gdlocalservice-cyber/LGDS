A professional tune-up keeps your door running quietly, safely, and efficiently - and helps catch small issues before they become costly repairs. Serving PA, NJ, and DE.

What's included in a tune-up

  • Full lubrication of springs, hinges, rollers, and tracks
  • Balance and tension adjustment
  • Hardware tightening - bolts, brackets, and fasteners
  • Safety reversal and auto-stop sensor test
  • Opener force and travel limit check
  • Visual inspection of cables, drums, and pulleys
  • Weatherstripping condition check

Signs your door needs a tune-up

  • Door is noisy - grinding, squeaking, or rattling
  • Door moves unevenly or jerks during operation
  • Door is slower than usual opening or closing
  • It's been more than 12 months since the last service
  • After a hard winter or extended heavy use

Tip: Annual maintenance can significantly extend the life of your springs, opener, and hardware.

Why regular maintenance matters

Your garage door is the largest moving part of your home - it opens and closes over 1,000 times a year. Without regular maintenance, springs wear faster, cables fray, and the opener works harder than it should.

A tune-up takes about 30–45 minutes and can prevent the most common (and most expensive) repairs.
