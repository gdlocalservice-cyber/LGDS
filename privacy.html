Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Local Garage Door Service (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or contact us.

1) Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, phone number, message).
  • Usage data such as pages viewed and device/browser info (via analytics tools).
  • Marketing data related to advertising performance (via Google Ads/Analytics).

2) How We Use Information

  • To respond to service requests and communicate with you.
  • To provide and improve our services and website experience.
  • To measure marketing performance and optimize advertising.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues.

SMS Policy

If you provide your phone number to contact us, request service, or as part of employment/technician onboarding, we may send SMS text messages related to appointments, job dispatch updates, technician notifications, and other operational communications.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or call 267-438-6494.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

3) Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website and communicate with customers (for example: form processing, analytics, and advertising tools), and when required by law.

4) Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to understand traffic and improve marketing effectiveness. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

5) Third-Party Services

  • Form submissions: Your request form is processed by Formspree.
  • Analytics/Ads: We use Google Analytics and Google Ads to understand website performance.
  • WhatsApp: If you click WhatsApp, WhatsApp may collect data under their policies.

6) Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7) Your Choices

  • You can request to update or delete your submitted information by calling 267-438-6494.
  • You can opt out of certain advertising personalization through Google’s ad settings.

8) Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9) Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

10) Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Call 267-438-6494.
